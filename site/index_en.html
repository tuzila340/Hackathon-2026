Preserving the land ecosystem is our shared future

Why is this important

The land ecosystem is the basis of life on Earth.

The terrestrial ecosystem is the foundation of life on our planet. Forests, steppes, mountains, deserts and soils provide us with air, water, food and a home for millions of species of living organisms. However, human activities - deforestation, pollution, overuse of natural resources - are putting this balance at risk.

Our goal

Together for nature conservation

Our website is designed to explain why preserving the terrestrial ecosystem is so important, what problems exist today, and what each of us can do to protect nature. Even small steps make a big difference.
