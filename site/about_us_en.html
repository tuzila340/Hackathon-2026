Who are we ?

Our team

We are a group of enthusiasts who strive to preserve nature.

We are a team of caring people united by a love for nature and a desire to preserve it for future generations. Our project has an educational goal - to raise awareness about the problems of the terrestrial ecosystem and show that everyone can contribute to its protection.

Our mission

Spread knowledge about terrestrial ecosystems

To draw attention to environmental problems To motivate people to act responsibly towards nature.
