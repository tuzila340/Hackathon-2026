Game

Learn while playing!

The essence of the game

The land ecosystem is the basis of life on Earth.

In this interactive game you become a real nature protector! Various pieces of garbage appear in front of you - leaves, bags and other objects. Your goal is to drag all the garbage to the bin. Each collected item adds points to your score.

The game helps to realize that even small actions, like cleaning up trash, can make the environment cleaner and safer for all living things.

How to play

The goal of the game

Learn to care for the terrestrial ecosystem and develop attentiveness and speed. Each attempt helps to understand the importance of small ecological actions and motivates to make the world cleaner and safer for nature.

  • Push and pull the trash to the bin.
  • Each item that falls into the urn adds +1 to the score.
